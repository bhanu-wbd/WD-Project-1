Trinity God

The following are the one and only one 'Trinity God':

The Father

'Yehova' is the'Father', who is a creator of the universe, land, water, creatures, and the humans.

The Son

'Jesus Christ' is the son of God, who came to this world to shed his holy blood, died on cross and resurrected on the third day to forgive all kind of sins of his beloved children.

The Holy Spirit

'Holy spirit' are the 7 sprits that are in front of the God's thrown in Heaven. These sprits help mankind to overcome all sorts of wickedness on this earth and help to live holy life as Jesus wants.
